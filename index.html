My Projects

Project Title: Building My Professional Portfolio Website

Overview: I’m embarking on a project to create a personal portfolio website using Stephen R. Covey’s “The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People” as a guide. This website will be my digital showcase, highlighting my achievements, skills, and potential for future career opportunities or educational advancements. By applying Covey’s habits, I’ll not only build a portfolio but also enhance my personal effectiveness and professionalism.

Objectives:

  • Design and develop my personal portfolio website.
  • Apply the 7 Habits of Highly Effective People to my personal and professional growth.
  • Present my skills, projects, and achievements in a clear and professional manner.
  • Reflect on what I’ve learned and how I’ve applied Covey’s habits.

Assignment Breakdown:

Habit 1: Be Proactive

Task 1: Research and Select a Web Development Platform or Tool.

Rationale: After some research, I’ve decided to use WordPress for my portfolio website. WordPress strikes a good balance between user-friendliness and customization options, making it perfect for creating a professional site without requiring deep coding knowledge. Choosing WordPress is a proactive step because it allows me to build a robust and attractive website.

Habit 2: Begin with the End in Mind

Task 2: Draft a Project Plan for My Website.

Goals:

  • Create an online presence that highlights my skills and projects.
  • Attract potential employers and clients by showcasing my professional accomplishments.
  • Use the website as a platform for continuous personal and professional growth.

Target Audience:

  • Potential employers looking for candidates with IT and cybersecurity skills.
  • Clients seeking IT project management and cybersecurity services.
  • Academic professionals and institutions interested in my work and research.

Critical Sections of the Site:

  1. Home: Brief introduction and professional tagline, highlights of key projects and skills, and links to other sections of the site.
  2. About: Detailed bio including my background, education, and career highlights, and my professional philosophy and values.
  3. Portfolio: Showcase of selected projects with descriptions, technologies used, and outcomes.
  4. Contact: Contact form for visitors to get in touch, and my email address for direct communication.

Content Plan:

  • Home: A warm welcome message, a brief introduction about who I am and what I do, and a few highlights of my most significant projects.
  • About: A detailed biography, my career journey, key accomplishments, and my professional philosophy.
  • Portfolio: A collection of my best projects, each with a description of the project’s goals, the technologies used, my role, and the outcomes. This section will also include screenshots and links to the projects.
  • Contact: A simple and intuitive contact form and my professional email address.
