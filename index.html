Building My Professional Portfolio Website

Overview: I’m creating a personal portfolio website inspired by Stephen R. Covey’s “The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People.” This site will showcase my achievements, skills, and potential, aiming to enhance my professional growth and effectiveness.

Objectives:

  • Develop a personal portfolio website.
  • Apply Covey’s habits to improve personal and professional effectiveness.
  • Clearly present skills, projects, and achievements.
  • Reflect on personal growth and application of Covey’s principles.

Assignment Breakdown:

  • Habit 1: Be Proactive – Chose WordPress for its ease of use and customization options to build a professional website.
  • Habit 2: Begin with the End in Mind – Planned the website to highlight skills, attract potential employers, and support ongoing growth.

Content Plan:

  • Home: Welcome message and introduction.
  • About: Detailed biography and professional philosophy.
  • Portfolio: Showcase of projects with descriptions and outcomes.
  • Contact: Contact form and email address.
