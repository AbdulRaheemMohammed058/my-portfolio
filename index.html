My Projects

Project Title: Building My Professional Portfolio Website

Overview:

I’m embarking on a project to create a personal portfolio website using Stephen R. Covey’s “The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People” as a guide. This website will be my digital showcase, highlighting my achievements, skills, and potential for future career opportunities or educational advancements.

By applying Covey’s habits, I’ll not only build a portfolio but also enhance my personal effectiveness and professionalism.

Objectives:

  • Design and develop my personal portfolio website.
  • Apply the 7 Habits of Highly Effective People to my personal and professional growth.
  • Present my skills, projects, and achievements in a clear and professional manner.
  • Reflect on what I’ve learned and how I’ve applied Covey’s habits.

Assignment Breakdown:

Habit 1: Be Proactive

Task 1: Research and Select a Web Development Platform or Tool

Rationale:

After some research, I’ve decided to use WordPress for my portfolio website. WordPress strikes a good balance between user-friendliness and customization options, making it perfect for creating a professional site without requiring deep coding knowledge.

Choosing WordPress is a proactive step because it allows me to build a robust and attractive website. I’ve signed up for an online course to learn the ins and outs of WordPress, including how to use plugins and themes to enhance my site. By taking this initiative, I’m ensuring I have the skills needed to make the most out of WordPress, which will help me create a strong portfolio that effectively showcases my abilities and achievements.

Habit 2: Begin with the End in Mind

Task 2: Draft a Project Plan for My Website

Goals:

  • To create an online presence that highlights my skills and projects.
  • To attract potential employers and clients by showcasing my professional accomplishments.
  • To use the website as a platform for continuous personal and professional growth.

Target Audience:

  • Potential employers looking for candidates with IT and cybersecurity skills.
  • Clients seeking IT project management and cybersecurity services.
  • Academic professionals and institutions interested in my work and research.

Critical Sections of the Site:

  • Home:
    • Brief introduction and professional tagline.
    • Highlights of key projects and skills.
    • Links to other sections of the site.
  • About:
    • Detailed bio including my background, education, and career highlights.
    • My professional philosophy and values.
    • Downloadable resume
  • Portfolio:
    • Showcase of selected projects with descriptions, technologies used, and outcomes.
    • Links to live projects or code repositories (e.g., GitHub).
  • Contact:
    • Contact form for visitors to get in touch.
    • Links to my professional social media profiles (e.g., LinkedIn, GitHub).
    • Email address for direct communication.

Content Plan:

  • Home: A warm welcome message, a brief introduction about who I am and what I do, and a few highlights of my most significant projects.
  • About: A detailed biography, my career journey, key accomplishments, and my professional philosophy.
  • Portfolio: A collection of my best projects, each with a description of the project’s goals, the technologies used, my role, and the outcomes. This section will also include screenshots and links to the projects.
  • Contact: A simple and intuitive contact form, links to my LinkedIn and GitHub profiles, and my professional email address.
